Chicken Tetrazzini

Description

An American classic, this dish is comfort food to a 'T', and a go-to for weeknight meals.

Ingredients

  • Cooked Spaghetti
  • 1 cup Cream of Mushroom
  • 1 cup Cream of Chicken
  • 1 cup Sour Cream (or greek yogurt)
  • 1/2 bag Frozen english peas
  • 1 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 Chicken Breasts (leftovers are great for this)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic
  • 1 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  • 2 tsp Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Coat 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk cream of chicken, cream of mushroom, chicken broth, sour cream, butter, salt, pepper, and garlic powder until combine. Gradually add peas, chicken, and spaghetti in.
  3. Pour and spread mixture into pan. Top with mozzerella, then cheddar, and then finally parmesan.
  4. Bake 45 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven, and let stand for 10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
